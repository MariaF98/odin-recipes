Sausage-Corn Chili Recipe

Sausage-Corn Chili

For a quick week-night meal, this chili--with sausage, kidney beans, corn, and just the right kick--can't be beat.

  • 1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy Dean® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed, drained
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • ⅔ cup picante sauce or salsa

Optional Toppings:

  • Shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Chopped cilantro

Steps:

  1. Cook sausage, onion and chili powder in large saucepan over medium heat 4-5 minutes or until onion is tender.
  2. Stir in water, beans, corn, tomato sauce and picante sauce. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Ladle into bowls. Add toppings, if desired.
